Who, What, Where, When, and Why?

The Darfur genocide occured in 2003 in Darfur and Khartoum and was spearheaded by the khartoum government,
it was a mass murder campaign against the darfurian people. The reason for
the attacks on the Darfurian people was that they wanted more autonmy but the Khartoum
government did not want to give it to them.

Response from the U.S.

In response to the events happening in Darfur, the U.S. government decided to help in the construction of 34
base camps in Darfur for the African Union Mission in Sudan.

Non-Governmental response.

The UN did not classify the Darfur genocide as an act of genocide, meaning that it would be against
international law to intervene in the conflict.
